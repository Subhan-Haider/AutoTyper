# Privacy Policy — GhostType AutoTyper

**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

GhostType AutoTyper (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection and use of your personal information.

### 1. Information Collection and Transmission
- **No Personal Data Collection**: GhostType AutoTyper does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) to external servers.
- **Local Operation only**: The App operates entirely on your local device. 

### 2. Clipboard Access
- The App requires access to your system clipboard to read the contents you wish to automate (typing simulation).
- This data is processed in-memory only while the "Start Typing" feature is active and is **never** uploaded, saved, or logged.

### 3. API Integrations
- If you choose to enable the **"Humanize"** feature, the App sends your text to the `subhan.tech` API to paraphrase it. This is purely for text processing and no user-identifiable data is attached to these requests.

### 4. Third-Party Services
- The App does not contain any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or advertisements.

### 5. Your Consent
- By using our software, you consent to our privacy policy.
